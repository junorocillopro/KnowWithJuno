Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 15, 2026 · Last updated: June 15, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how KnowWithJuno, operated by Juno Rocillo, licensed FWD Life Insurance Financial Partner, collects, uses, and protects the personal information you provide through our website, coverage quiz, and Facebook or Instagram lead forms.

1. Information We Collect

We collect personal information when you:

  • Complete the KnowWithJuno Coverage Gap Quiz at knowwithjuno.com
  • Submit a lead form on our Facebook or Instagram pages
  • Send us a message or email directly

The information we may collect includes: full name, email address, phone number, financial information you choose to share (monthly income, current coverage, dependents) through the quiz only, and your responses to qualifying questions on our lead forms.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide solely to: deliver your coverage gap results, contact you to schedule a Gap Review or Clarity Session, send follow-up information relevant to your financial protection needs, and respond to your messages and questions. We do not use your information for automated decision-making beyond matching you with relevant insurance coverage options.

3. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties. Your information may be shared with the following service providers strictly for operating our follow-up and communication systems: GoHighLevel (CRM and email automation), Make.com (workflow automation), Notion (lead tracking), Calendly (appointment scheduling), and Meta/Facebook (lead form data collection via Facebook Lead Ads). All providers are required to handle your data securely.

4. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to provide our services — typically no longer than 24 months unless you become a client of Juno Rocillo, in which case records are retained in accordance with FWD Life Insurance compliance requirements.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to request access to, correct, or delete your personal information, and to withdraw consent to receive communications from us at any time. To exercise these rights, contact us at the email address below.

6. Data Security

We take reasonable precautions to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. All data collected through our quiz and lead forms is transmitted securely.

7. Facebook and Instagram Lead Forms

When you submit information through a Facebook or Instagram lead ad, your data is collected by Meta Platforms and shared with us in accordance with Meta's own Privacy Policy. By submitting a lead form, you consent to us receiving and using that information to contact you regarding your insurance needs.
